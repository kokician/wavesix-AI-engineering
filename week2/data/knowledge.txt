Welcome to Cosmo Clothing — your trusted fashion destination.

Shipping Information:
- We offer standard shipping (3–5 business days) and express shipping (1–2 business days).
- Orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping.
- You can track your order using the order ID provided in your confirmation email.

Returns & Refunds:
- You can return unworn items within 30 days of delivery.
- Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the returned item.
- To initiate a return, contact our support team or use the returns portal on our website.

Product Info:
- All our hoodies are made with organic cotton.
- We carry sizes from XS to XXL in most categories.
- New seasonal collections drop every 3 months — Spring (March), Summer (June), Fall (September), Winter (December).

Support Hours:
- Our live chat and phone support are available from 9am to 6pm (GMT+2), Monday to Saturday.
- Email support is available 24/7 at support@cosmoclothing.com

Common Questions:
- Q: How can I cancel my order?
  A: Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placing them by using the cancellation link in your email.
  
- Q: Do you ship internationally?
  A: Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping fees and times vary by destination.
  
- Q: Can I exchange an item?
  A: Yes, we offer size and style exchanges if the item is returned in original condition.
